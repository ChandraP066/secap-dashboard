About this prototype
v0.3 · synthetic data · 2026
This dashboard visualises the operational case made in Paneru, C. P. (2025), Smart Energy Applications and the Sustainable Energy Transition in Norway: Planning Implications and Policy Perspectives, PhD Thesis, University of Stavanger. ISBN 978-82-8439-405-3.

All data shown is synthetic but grounded: household counts, SEA penetration, and engagement ranges reflect aggregated findings from 7 999 app store reviews (Paper III), survey responses from 573 Norwegian households (Paper V), and sectoral analysis (Paper VI). Regression coefficients used in the Demand Response Simulator derive from Paper V's structural equation model.

Gap typology (A–D) follows the thesis problem statement. Peer-city comparisons, district boundaries, and feed items are illustrative and should not be cited as empirical findings.

Built as a portfolio prototype by Chandra Prakash Paneru. Not operational · not endorsed by Stavanger kommune.
